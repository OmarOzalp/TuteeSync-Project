Committed to helping improve education worldwide

My brother and I in Cairo International Airport.

Hey everyone, my name is Omar Ozalp and I'm a senior in high school. I spent my first ten years in Egypt and the rest of my life in the UAE. When I moved, the contrast amazed me and I realized the potential that could be achieved in Egypt if systems worked better. After a lot of thought, I believe that a weak education system is one of the root causes of my countries problems. I became obsessed with improving education, specifically through computer science.

CS is a precious tool, with websites like Khan Academy transforming my educational experience throughout high school. My dream is to tackle these global educational problems, eventually transforming the system in my home country. My first target was the UAE, although it has a robust education system, one weakness I found was within tutoring. Currently, there is no easy way to find tutors in the country. Usually, people just ask around in search for tutors. This is not only extremely time-consuming, but poses a risk of issues during the process. I also noticed that this led to everyone using the same tutors, making it not only difficult for others to find clients but also allowing for the monopolization of the industry. For my first project, I will attempt to tackle this tutoring issue in the UAE. While this website is still in its beginning phases, I plan to get it up and running by April of 2024, after getting more experience in back-end web development. Through my time in university, I want to interact, collaborate, and learn from the diverse group of people around me to continue building new projects that help improve education worldwide.
